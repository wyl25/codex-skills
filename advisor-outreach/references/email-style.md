# Email Style

The goal is a thoughtful, individualized outreach email, not an official self-recommendation template.

## Tone

- Polite, sincere, concrete, and restrained.
- Use "我注意到", "我对...很感兴趣", "这与我目前...经历形成了自然衔接" instead of exaggerated praise.
- Prefer specific research connection over generic admiration.
- Keep paragraphs shorter than the user's raw template.
- Avoid stacking all awards in one long paragraph.

## Structure

Subject:

`27保研自荐-王艺量-南京信息工程大学人工智能专业`

Possible variants:

- `27保研自荐-王艺量-医学图像分析方向`
- `保研咨询-王艺量-南京信息工程大学人工智能学院`

Email body:

1. Greeting.
2. Identity and strongest academic facts.
3. Research experience most relevant to the advisor.
4. Advisor-specific fit paragraph.
5. Why the user wants to continue in this direction.
6. Attachment note and request for further communication.
7. Polite close.

## Personalization Rules

For every advisor, include at least one advisor-specific anchor:

- A recent paper topic.
- A lab direction.
- A project or platform.
- A method/data/application overlap.

Then connect one user-specific anchor:

- TMI under-review vessel segmentation work.
- Weakly supervised 3D vessel segmentation.
- Multimodal brain imaging patent.
- AI/algorithm competitions.
- Strong GPA/rank/course performance.

## Avoid

- "学术长青" unless the user explicitly wants a very traditional close.
- "突出的学术成果" without specific evidence.
- "认真研读了您的论文" unless the user actually did.
- Overclaiming paper status: use "在投/Under Review" when applicable.
- Reusing the same advisor-fit paragraph with only the name changed.
